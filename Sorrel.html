Sorrel Blackbeak

Image

Sorrel Blackbeak is an Ironteeth witch of the Blackbeak Clan and a fierce member of The Thirteen, known for her loyalty and combat skills...

Physical Description

Sorrel has dark brown hair, gold-flecked black eyes, and a tan complexion, embodying the fierce spirit of her clan...

Personality Traits

She is bold, fiercely loyal, and unyielding in battle, often acting as a protector for her friends and allies...

Magical Abilities

Sorrel possesses the typical abilities of an Ironteeth witch, including enhanced strength and the ability to ride wyverns...

Connections

Sorrel has a close bond with Manon Blackbeak and the other members of The Thirteen, standing by them through thick and thin...

Spoiler Alert

Sorrel is dead. She sacrificed herself to save Terrasen.
