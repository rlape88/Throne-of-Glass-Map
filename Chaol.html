Chaol Westfall

Physical Description

Chaol is a 24 year old human male. He has chestnut hair with bronze colored eyes. He stands at 6'1" and has a distinct scar on his face.

He also has a wheelchair that he sometimes has to use, due to his spinal injury that wasn't able to be fully healed by Yrene.

Origin

Annielle, Adarlan

Connections

Chaol is married to Yrene, and they are expecting a child. His father is Lord Westfall. His mother is Lady Westfall. His brother is Terrin Westfall.

Titles

Lord of Anielle

Captain of the Royal Guard (formerly)

Hand of the King

Alias

Lord Westfall

Spoiler Alert

Chaol is alive. His life is bound to Yrene's life.
