Elide Lochan

Physical Description

Elide is a 19 year old human female. She has black hair, black/onyx eyes, and pale white skin. She stands at 5'2" and has scars on her ankle from a previous break when she was younger.

Origin

Perranth, Terrasen

Connections

Elide is mated and married to Lorchan Salvaterre. She comes from the House Lochan. She has Ironteeth Witches in her bloodline, which comes from her mother.

Elide's mom, Marion Lochan, died saving Aelin as a child, and her father, Cal Lochan, was later executed for being a Lord of Terrasen. Her uncle was Vernon Lochan, and he was her guardian after her parents died.

While Elide has no definitive magic, she has a special connection with the goddess Anneith.

Titles

Lady of Perranth

Spoiler Alert

Elide is alive.
