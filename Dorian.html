Dorian Havilliard

Physical Description

Dorian is a 20 year old human male. He has black hair with sapphire eyes. His height is 6'0". He has a white band-like scar that goes around his throat.

Origin

Rifthold, Adarlan

Dorian possesses powerful magic, particularly the ability to manipulate light and shadow...

Connections

Dorian is in a relationship with Manon Blackbeak. He had a former lover, Sorscha, who was beheaded by his father, The King (while possessed by Erawan).

His deceased father is Dorian Havilliard I. His mother is Georgiana Havilliard. His younger brother is Hollin Havilliard.

His cousins and distant relatives are Roland Havilliard, Aelin Galathynius, Lidia Cervos, Brann, and Actaeon.

Some of his notable ancestors are Gavin Havilliard, Elena Galathynius, and Brannon Galathynius.
