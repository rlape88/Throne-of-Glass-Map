Asterin Blackbeak

Physical Description

Asterin is a female ironteeth witch with blonde hair. Her eyes are black with gold flecks spread throughout. She is well over 100 years old.

Origin

Witch Kingdom

Wyvern

Asterin is bonded to a wyvern named Narene.

Magical Abilities

Asterin's power is simply The Yielding, like all witches. They can have one big blast of power when they are close to dying. This power can wipe out large armies and is typically used as a last resort.

Connections

Asterin had a relationship with 'The Hunter' who died of old age. She also had a stillborn witchling. She is the second in command of the Thirteen, which is lead by Manon Blackbeak. She is apart of the Blackbeak Clan.

Title

Second

Spoiler Alert

Asterin is dead. She sacrificed herself to save Erilea by doing the Yielding.
