Fenrys Moonbeam

Physical Description

Fenrys is a 137 year old male fae. His second form is a white wolf. He has golden hair with onyx eyes and golden brown skin. He also has distinct twin scars on his face. His height is 6'3" and he appears as if he is 25 years old.

Origin

Wendyn, Doranelle

Magical Abilities

Fenrys possesses unique abilities that allow him to transport himself and others, showcasing his powerful Fae heritage...

Connections

Fenrys is now blood-sworn to Aelin Ashryver Whitethorn Galathynius. He used to be apart of Maeve's Cadre and blood-sworn to Maeve. He had a twin brother, Connall, who was murdered by Maeve while Fenrys and Aelin were captured.

Titles

The White Wolf of Doranelle

Ambassador of Terrasen
