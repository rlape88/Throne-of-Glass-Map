Manon Blackbeak Crochan

Physical Description

Manon is a 117 year old female witch. She has white hair, gold eyes, and pale white skin. Her distinct features are the gold eyes, white hair, iron nails, and ironteeth. She stands at 5'8" and appears to be 26 years old physically.

Origins

Witch Kingdom

Half Ironteeth & Half Crochan

Wyvern

Manon is bonded to a small dark grey wyvern named Abraxos from Morath. Who has spidersilk wings, ironteeth, and iron spikes on his tail.

Magical Abilities

Manon possesses powerful witch magic, including the ability to control and communicate with her wyvern...

Connections

Manon is the leader of The Thirteen. She is apart of both the Blackbeak Clan and Crochan Clan. She is in an undefined relationship with Dorian Havilliard.

Her mother is Lothian Blackbeak, and her father is Tristan Crochan. She has a half-sister through their father, Rhiannon Crochan II. Her grandmother is the Blackbeak Matron, and her great-grandmother is Glennis Crochan. Her cousins are Asterin Blackbeak and Bronwen Crochan. She has two distant cousins Edda Blackbeak and Briar Blackbeak.

Manon is descended from The Valg and The Fae, like all witches are.
