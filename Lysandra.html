Lysandra Ennar

Physical Description

Lysandra is a 20 year old female human. She has dark brown hair with emerald green eyes. Her skin is sun-kissed. She stands at 5'4".

Her preferred shifted form is a ghost leopard.

Origin

Adarlan

Magical Abilities

Lysandra is a shapeshifter, able to transform into various animals, including her preferred form of a ghost leopard...

Connections

Lysandra is mated and married to Aedion Ashryver. She has an adopted daughter, Evangeline. She used to be owned by Arobynn Hammel and worked for Clarisse DuVency. The house she is apart of is House Caraverre. Her uncle is Falkan Ennar.

Her cousin-in-laws are Aelin Galathynius and Rowan Whitethorn

Titles

Lady of Caraverre

Aliases

Shifter

Lysandra is alive.
