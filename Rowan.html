Rowan Whitethorn

Physical Description

Rowan is a 350 year old fae male. His second form is a hawk. His hair is silver, his eyes are green, and his skin is tan. He has very distinctive tattoos that go up the side of his neck and face. He stands at 6'4" and physically looks to be 25-28 years old.

It is noted that he has a distinct smell of Pine & Snow.

Origin

Wendyn, Doranelle

Magical Abilities

Rowan possesses powerful magic, including the ability to manipulate fire and heal others. His skills are enhanced by his Fae heritage, making him a formidable ally in battle.

Connections

Rowan is mated and married to Aelin Galathynius. He was previously widowed to his presumed mate, Lyria, who was killed by Maeve while carrying their unborn child.

He is connected to the House Whitethorn and House Galathynius.

His parents are unknown. His super great-grandmother is Mora. He is very distantly related to Aedion Ashryver and House Ashryver. His uncle is Ellys Whitethorn. He has two known cousins, Sellene and Endymion Whitethorn.

He is blood-sworn to Aelin Galathynius and is apart of Aelin's Cadre. He was formerly blood-sworn to Maeve and apart of Maeve's Cadre.

Titles

Aliases
