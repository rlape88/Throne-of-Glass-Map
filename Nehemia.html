Nehemia Ytger

Physical Description

Nehemia was a 19 year old female human. She had black hair, brown eyes, and dark skin.

Origin

Banjali, Eyllwe

Magical Abilities

Nehemia is skilled in Wyrdmarks, using her knowledge to aid her friends and fight against oppression...

Connections

Nehemia is apart of her family House Ytger. She a member of The Rebels while she was in Adarlan. She worked with Elena Galathynius Havilliard to prepare Aelin Galathynius for her future role. She was also friends with Aelin while she was parading as Liliana.

Her parents are the King and Queen of Eyllwe. Her brothers are Kharis and Deji Ytger.

Titles

Princess of Eyllwe

Light of Eyllwe

Spoiler Alert

Nehemia is dead. She was killed in order to start Aelin onto her path to save Erilea.
