Aelin Ashryver Galathynius

Physical Description

Aelin is a 20 year old female fae. She has striking features, including golden hair and turquoise eyes ringed with gold. Those eyes are also known as 'Ashryver eyes'.

Magical Abilities

Aelin Ashryver Whitethorn Galathynius possesses a remarkable array of magical abilities that make her one of the most powerful character.
